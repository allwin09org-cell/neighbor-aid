About Neighbor Aid

What Neighbor Aid does

Neighbor Aid connects a local community through a simple web platform. One person can post a request for help, and other people can respond through comments on that request's detail page.

Mission

Support people quickly

Neighbor Aid aims to make local support simple, fast, and easy to access so people can ask for help and receive a response from nearby neighbors.

Vision

Build a caring local network

Neighbor Aid wants to grow into a trusted space where communities stay connected, support one another, and respond together during daily needs and urgent situations.
